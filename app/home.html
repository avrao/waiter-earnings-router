Explanation of how it works. 'New-Meal' 
tab takes the Waiter who is calculating the 
total price of the meal enters the base. Waiter 
is required to enter price of the meal, tax 
rate and tip percentage. When the submit button
is hit, the box below with header, 'Customer 
Charges' populates with subtotal, tip and 
total price for the meal.Multiple meal total
can be calculated. User may hit cancel to undo
any mistyped numbers.
	My-Earnings tab is also populated with
waiters tip, along with meal count and also
gives the average tip per meal. Hitting reset
button clears all the entries in New-Meal tab 
as well as My-Earnings tab
